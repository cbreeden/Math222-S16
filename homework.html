About

Here you can find the schedule for all homework assignments in this course. Remember, late homework is not accepted. If you find that you will not be able to make it to discussion on the given due date, try to work out arrangments with me to turn in your homework early either by handing it in on a preceeding discussion session or turning your homework in my mail-box. Please bare in mind though, that whenever you hand your homework in using unconventional methods the more likely it is for me to misplace it; so I recommend trying your best to turn it in on the due date listed in discussion.
