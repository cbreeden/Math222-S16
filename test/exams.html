About

Here you can find the schedule for all of the quizzes and exams in this course. Remember, there are no makeup quizzes, but the two lowest quiz scores will be dropped.

I will post the quizzes and exams after every student has taken it. I will also post try to post solutions. This will be a very good study resource as the final exam will consist of problems very similar to the ones found here; so if you do nothing else in this course, at least make sure you understand these problems very well.

The University requires that you take the final exam at the scheduled time shown above. For any conflict with midterms, please let me and Professor Gong know two weeks prior to the exam. Make-up exams will cover the same topics as the regular exams, although they may look completely different.
